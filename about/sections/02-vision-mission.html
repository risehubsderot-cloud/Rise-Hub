Transforming Sderot into a global catalyst for health & resilience

"Where the frontline of crisis becomes the frontier of breakthrough technologies."

The Western Negev region represents a unique living laboratory. The challenges encountered here demand rapid, robust solutions. By building a unified resilience tech platform, we convert stress into scalable, investable assets that address clinical and community needs across the globe.

Our Vision

Building an innovative ecosystem where startups, corporations, academic institutions, and government work together to realize breakthrough technologies for human & national resilience, coming from Sderot to the entire world.

Our Mission

Transforming Sderot and the Western Negev into a global hub for innovation—advancing health and resilience while generating tangible value for communities locally and globally.

Why Here?

Sderot serves as a ripe environment for the advancement of next-generation resilience technologies — trauma management, community recovery, and data-driven emotional support. It offers unparalleled business opportunities and fosters many potential collaborations.
